Welcome to our web-app designed to improve the quality of life of patients undergoing chemotherapy

Our app is designed to provide patients with access to important information and resources to help them manage their treatment and improve their well-being.

With our app, patients can access a variety of resources, including nutritional guidance, tips for managing side effects, administrative support, and psycho-oncology resources. Each section of the app is designed to provide patients with the tools and information they need to navigate their treatment journey with confidence.

Our app is designed with the needs of patients in mind. It is easy to use and accessible from any device with an internet connection. Whether you are at home or on the go, you can access the information you need to support your treatment and improve your quality of life.

We believe that access to information and resources is key to improving the quality of life of patients undergoing chemotherapy. That's why we created this app - to provide patients with the support they need to thrive during their treatment. We hope that you find our app helpful and informative, and we welcome your feedback and suggestions for how we can continue to improve and grow.
